Utah Transportation and Public Safety – Crash Data Initiative (UTAPS-CDI)

    Our research group manages motor vehicle crash records in the state of Utah through UTAPS-CDI, a partnership between the University of Utah and two main supporting agencies: Utah Department of Transportation (UDOT) and the Utah Department of Public Safety – Highway Safety Office (HSO). UTAPS-CDI unifies the management of crash data for the State of Utah.

    • Back-end database solutions
    • Middleware abstraction layer
    • Web interfaces to process, display, conduct quality control, produce analytic tools, and provide data and technical services
  • Unified source of motor vehicle crashes in Utah
  • Streamlines workflow for partners
  • Improves data consistency and service
  • Enhances reporting and analytics
  • Leverages data to improve safety through research
